Common issues I help with

Tell me what you’re seeing—these are typical warning signs:

  • Musty odor, high humidity, condensation
  • Standing water or damp areas after rain
  • Efflorescence (white powder) on basement walls
  • Cracks, sticking doors, uneven floors (symptoms)
  • Crawlspace moisture, sagging insulation, wood concerns

Note: This site is for homeowner consulting and education. If you also perform repairs, adjust wording accordingly.

Services

Structural & Foundation Symptom Review

Walk-through of cracks, settlement symptoms, floor issues, and drainage factors— with a practical explanation of what’s normal vs. what needs action.

Moisture Control Assessment

Identify moisture sources and pathways (air, soil, plumbing, exterior water), then outline ventilation, sealing, and humidity-control steps that make sense.

Water Seepage & Drainage Strategy

Evaluate seepage patterns and rain events, gutters/downspouts, grading, and likely entry points. Get a prioritized plan before paying for repairs.

Deliverables you’ll receive

  • Short written summary of findings (plain English)
  • Prioritized “Fix First / Fix Next / Monitor” list
  • Photo notes of problem areas
  • Questions to ask contractors (to prevent over-selling)

What I’m not

  • Not an engineer’s stamped report (when you need that, I’ll tell you)
  • Not a substitute for a licensed contractor’s scope of work
  • Not guesswork—I focus on visible evidence + practical steps
